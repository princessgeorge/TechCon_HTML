Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of innovators and has since grown into one of the leading technology conferences worldwide. Each year, it brings together thousands of developers, entrepreneurs, and thought leaders.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and empower the next generation of technology leaders. We strive to create an inclusive space where bold ideas can flourish.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Renowned as the world’s first computer programmer, Ada Lovelace has inspired countless innovators with her vision of what computing could become.

Alan Turing

Pioneer of modern computing and artificial intelligence, Alan Turing’s work laid the foundation for the digital era we live in today.

Grace Hopper

A trailblazer in computer programming, Grace Hopper developed key programming languages and inspired diversity in technology.
